Favorite Movie

Money Heist

My favorite movie is Money Heist because it’s full of suspense, clever strategies,
and unexpected twists that keep me hooked. I admire how the characters aren’t just thieves, but people with deep stories and emotions.
The way the Professor plans everything with precision makes the story thrilling, and the teamwork, loyalty, and sacrifice shown by
the group make it more than just a heist—it’s about family and resistance.
