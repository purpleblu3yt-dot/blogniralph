My Favorite Anime


DEMON SLAYER

Demon Slayer is one of my favorite anime because of its amazing animation, exciting battles, and emotional storyline. I like it because the characters are inspiring, especially Tanjiro, who shows kindness, courage, and determination even in difficult situations.
The bond between the siblings and the friendships built along the way make the story meaningful, while the sword fights and visuals make it thrilling to watch.
